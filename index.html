Features for Every User

Clients

  • 🔍Provider & Service Discovery: View hairstylist profiles and browse services.
  • 📅Advanced Booking: Select services, add-ons, variations, and book with an interactive calendar.
  • 📍Flexible Location: Choose service at home or provider's location.
  • 📝Booking Summary: Review all details before confirming.
  • 💳Payments & Scheduling: Pay securely, view appointment history, receive notifications, and reschedule.

Providers

  • 🛠️Service & Profile Management: Create, edit, and delete services with complex pricing and image galleries.
  • 🔗Shareable Profile: Share a unique booking link with clients.
  • ⏰Availability & Appointments: Manage calendar, accept/reject requests, and propose new times.
  • 💸Payments & Subscriptions: Connect bank account, receive payouts, and manage premium plans.

Shared

  • 👤Account & Profile: Secure registration, login, password recovery, and profile updates.
  • 💬Real-time Communication: 1:1 chat and push notifications for messages and appointments.
  • 🛡️Safety & Moderation: Block or report users for safety.
  • 🌐Multilingual: Switch between English and French.
